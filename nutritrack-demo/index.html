How it works with the agent

Through an Openclaw chat, you tell the AI agent what you ate — in plain language or by sending a photo of the meal. The agent estimates calories, protein, carbs, and fat, then logs the entry and reports the updated totals back to the dashboard on the left. Same flow for weight, activity, and health readings.
