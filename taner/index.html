01 / About

a short orbit

— origins, chemistry, and what came next

Born in the Danube Delta, grew up on the Bosphorus — shifting daily between Europe and Asia — and found a home in the Nordics.

Background in Computer Engineering, but most of my working years went into Analytical Chemistry. Chemistry was one of the ways I tried to read the language of the universe.

Lately I get to combine both — working at a research center where research meets AI and digitalization. I follow AI developments closely, and I'm quietly fascinated by blockchain as an idea.

Off the clock, I love to camp and cycle with the family. Once upon a time I was an enthusiast amateur GO player — still, in my books, the universe's best game so far.

And I wear (and make) designs for the things I like. — links are hidden above :)
